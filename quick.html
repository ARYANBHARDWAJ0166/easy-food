Welcome to Quick Vegan Recipes!

Discover easy and delicious vegan recipes that you can whip up in no time. Perfect for students and anyone looking for quick meal options!

Vegan Stir-Fry

Ingredients: Broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, tofu, soy sauce, garlic.

Instructions: Stir-fry the vegetables and tofu in a pan with a bit of oil. Add soy sauce and garlic for flavor. Serve hot!

Chickpea Salad

Ingredients: Chickpeas, cucumber, tomatoes, onion, olive oil, lemon juice.

Instructions: Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice. Toss and enjoy!

Vegan Tacos

Ingredients: Corn tortillas, black beans, avocado, salsa, lettuce.

Instructions: Fill tortillas with black beans, avocado, and salsa. Top with fresh lettuce and enjoy!
