Quinoa Salad

Ingredients: Quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, parsley, lemon juice, olive oil.

Instructions: Cook quinoa, let it cool, and mix with diced vegetables. Drizzle with lemon juice and olive oil.

Avocado Toast

Ingredients: Whole grain bread, ripe avocados, salt, pepper, lemon juice, chili flakes.

Instructions: Toast bread, mash avocado with seasoning, and spread on toast.
