Grilled Veggie Tacos

Ingredients: Bell peppers, onions, zucchini, tortillas, avocado, salsa.

Instructions: Grill the vegetables until tender. Warm the tortillas and fill them with veggies, avocado, and salsa.

Pasta Primavera

Ingredients: Pasta, cherry tomatoes, garlic, spinach, parmesan, olive oil.

Instructions: Cook pasta, sauté garlic and tomatoes in olive oil, add spinach, and mix everything with pasta. Top with parmesan.

Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients: Bell peppers, quinoa, black beans, corn, tomatoes, cheese.

Instructions: Hollow out bell peppers and stuff them with a mixture of quinoa, beans, corn, and tomatoes. Bake at 375°F for 25 minutes, top with cheese.
