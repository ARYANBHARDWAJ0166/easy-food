Easy Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil a pot of salted water and add the pasta. Cook according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and saut√© until golden.
  3. Drain the pasta and add it to the pan. Toss with garlic and olive oil.
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Serve with fresh basil on top.

Grilled Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • Butter for grilling
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place cheese, tomato, and cucumber between two slices of bread.
  3. Heat a pan and grill the sandwich until golden and crispy on both sides.
  4. Serve hot with your favorite sauce.

Simple Omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1/4 cup chopped onions, tomatoes, or cheese

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add salt and pepper, and beat them well.
  2. Heat a pan over medium heat and melt the butter.
  3. Pour the beaten eggs into the pan, and cook for a few minutes until the eggs start to set.
  4. Optionally, add onions, tomatoes, or cheese on one side of the omelette.
  5. Fold the omelette in half and cook for another minute.
  6. Serve hot with toast or a salad.

Fruit Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk (or almond milk)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)

Instructions

  1. Peel the banana and cut it into chunks.
  2. Add the banana, frozen berries, yogurt, milk, and honey (if using) to a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour into a glass and enjoy your refreshing smoothie!
